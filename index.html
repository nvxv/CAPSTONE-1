The Capital of Caps and Stones

Welcome to the Cap Store of Capstone, the capital city of Capland.

   Here we sell caps of all kinds, for your bottles or your heads.

    Cap Store is one of the earliest equipment makers dating back to the 1880’s, and had a major share of the market up to the 1950's. During the 1940’s war years, caps were manufactured mostly in Capland. Capone & Caprice was a distributor of Cap Store caps for a time until around the 1950's. By 1949 or 1950, The Caplite Company acquired Cap Store's capmaking business. Cap Store continued supplying MLB caps made under private label, probably by American Capper, until the late 1950's. Cap supplied flannel wool jerseys into the early 1970's, and made some of the first double knit jerseys to be worn on-field, but was gone from the MLB jersey market by 1973.
